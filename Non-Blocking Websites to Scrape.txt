Non-Blocking Websites to Scrape

Quotes to Scrape
https://quotes.toscrape.com/
— Made exactly for scraping practice. No blocks, simple HTML.

Books to Scrape
https://books.toscrape.com/
— Fake online bookstore, perfect for beginners.

httpbin
https://httpbin.org/
— Useful for testing HTTP requests and responses.

Fake Store API (with frontend)
https://fakestoreapi.com/
— Fake e-commerce API with product data.

OpenWeatherMap (free pages)
https://openweathermap.org/city/2172797
— Weather info pages, simple and public.

Wikipedia
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Main_Page
— Public info, large site but polite scraping rules.

News sites like CNN or BBC (for headlines)
https://www.cnn.com/
— Just headlines and summary, light scraping.
